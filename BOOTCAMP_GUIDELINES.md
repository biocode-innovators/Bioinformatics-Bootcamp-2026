# 🧬 Bootcamp Guidelines

Welcome to the **1-Month Bioinformatics Bootcamp 2026** organized by **BioCode Innovators**.

To ensure a productive and collaborative learning environment, all participants are requested to follow the guidelines below throughout the bootcamp.

---

## Professional Conduct

Participants are expected to maintain respectful and professional communication with instructors and fellow learners at all times.

---

## Attendance

- Attend live sessions whenever possible.
- If you are unable to attend, watch the lecture recording before the next session.
- Active participation is highly encouraged throughout the bootcamp.

---

## Learning Approach

This bootcamp emphasizes:

- Conceptual understanding
- Critical thinking
- Scientific reasoning
- Continuous learning

Participants are encouraged to ask questions, participate in discussions, and explore additional learning resources.

---

## Learning Resources

Official learning materials, lecture recordings, announcements, and assessments will be shared through this GitHub repository and the official communication channels of BioCode Innovators.

---

## Academic Integrity

Participants are expected to complete all assessments independently.

Please avoid plagiarism and ensure that all submitted work reflects your own understanding.

---

## Community Standards

We encourage an inclusive and supportive learning environment.

Please:

- Respect everyone's opinions.
- Maintain professional communication.
- Support fellow learners.
- Contribute positively to discussions.

---

## Communication

Official announcements regarding lectures, assessments, and bootcamp updates will be shared through the designated communication channels.

Participants are encouraged to stay updated regularly.

---

## Our Goal

This bootcamp has been designed to provide a strong theoretical foundation in Bioinformatics and inspire learners to continue exploring Computational Biology through research, collaboration, and lifelong learning.

Thank you for being part of the BioCode Innovators learning community.

**Happy Learning! 🧬**
