# 🧬 Lecture 02 — Biological Databases

This lecture introduces biological databases and explains their significance in storing, organizing, and retrieving biological information for research and scientific discovery.

### Topics Covered

- Introduction to Biological Databases
- Classification of Biological Databases
- Primary Databases
- Secondary Databases
- Composite Databases
- Importance of Biological Databases
- Major Public Biological Databases
- Applications in Modern Biological Research

Participants will gain an understanding of how biological databases support genomics, proteomics, molecular biology, and computational research.
