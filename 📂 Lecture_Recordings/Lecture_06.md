# 🧬 Lecture 06 – Sequence Analysis

This lecture introduced the principles and methodologies of **Sequence Analysis**, enabling participants to compare biological sequences, identify similarities and differences, and infer functional, structural, and evolutionary relationships. Participants explored the significance of sequence alignment and learned how sequence comparison supports modern biological research.

## 📚 Topics Covered

- Introduction to Sequence Analysis
- Importance of Sequence Analysis in Bioinformatics
- Pairwise Sequence Alignment
- Multiple Sequence Alignment (MSA)
- Global vs. Local Sequence Alignment
- Sequence Similarity and Homology
- Conserved Regions, Motifs, and Functional Domains
- Biological Applications of Sequence Analysis
- Tools and Resources for Sequence Alignment

By the end of this lecture, participants developed a solid understanding of sequence comparison techniques and their applications in identifying homologous sequences, predicting gene and protein functions, studying evolutionary relationships, and supporting biological research.
