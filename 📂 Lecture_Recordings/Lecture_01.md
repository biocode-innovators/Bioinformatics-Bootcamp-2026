# 🧬 Lecture 01 — Introduction to Bioinformatics

This lecture provides a comprehensive introduction to Bioinformatics and establishes the foundation for the remainder of the bootcamp.

### Topics Covered

- What is Bioinformatics?
- History and Evolution of Bioinformatics
- Scope of Bioinformatics
- Major Applications in Modern Biology
- Career Opportunities in Bioinformatics
- Importance of Bioinformatics in Research and Healthcare

By the end of this lecture, participants will understand how Bioinformatics integrates biology with computational sciences to solve complex biological problems.
