## About the Bootcamp

The **Bioinformatics Bootcamp 2026** is a one-month educational initiative organized by **BioCode Innovators** to introduce students, researchers, educators, and professionals to the fundamentals of Bioinformatics.

The bootcamp is designed to build a strong theoretical foundation before participants move towards practical applications in computational biology and related disciplines.

This repository serves as the official learning hub for the bootcamp and will be continuously updated throughout the program.

# Bootcamp Curriculum

Throughout this bootcamp, participants will explore

- Introduction to Bioinformatics
- Biological Databases
- Protein Databases
- Data Retrieval
- DNA Sequence Analysis
- Sequence Analysis
- Pairwise Sequence Alignment
- Multiple Sequence Alignment
- Protein Sequence–Structure Relationships
- Bio-Computing
- Introduction to Molecular Docking

---

# Assessment Policy

Participants are encouraged to

- Think critically
- Write answers in their own words
- Demonstrate conceptual understanding
- Support answers with examples where appropriate

Outstanding submissions may be featured by **BioCode Innovators** with the participant's permission.

---

# Certificate Requirements

To receive an official certificate, participants must

- Maintain a minimum of 70% attendance
- Complete all required assessments
- Submit the feedback form
